Programming Festival

The Programming Festival will be an event filled with unique positivity and knowledge. For lovers of coding, it will be a real masterpiece, and for those who are not familiar with the IT world of programmers, they will get to know a lot of new and exciting things!


The festival consists of two categories:

The first part and category of this festival is music and celebration with friends, meeting new people, and the festival's opening. It starts while it's still warm and the sun is shining. After sunset, the second part of the festival begins, which focuses on programming, sharing knowledge, and enjoyment. Many professors and IT professionals will hold a speech.

When?

It will take place on July 23rd, starting at 7 pm and continuing until the same time the next day. This event is expected to last for 24 hours!

Price and Tickets:

Tickets will cost $300 for a single person.


Free Tickets!


Free tickets will be offered to the first 1,000 registered students with a GPA over 9.5 who are majoring in technology fields.

Where?

The upcoming festival will be located in the gardens of the campus at Stanford University. It's an enjoyable place where a large number of students will be part of the festival.

A beautiful stage will host both seasoned and contemporary musicians, all of whom are very well known. The opening act will feature the most popular artists!
